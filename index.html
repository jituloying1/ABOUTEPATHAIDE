🚖 About Pathaide

Pathaide is a modern mobility platform designed to make transportation simple, accessible, and reliable for everyone. We connect people with cars, bikes, e-rickshaws, and tractors for both travel and goods transport.

🌾 Rural & Village Focus

We focus on village and semi-urban areas where transportation is limited. Our goal is to connect rural communities with reliable transport services.

🌍 Our Mission

To build a strong and accessible transport network that reaches every corner, especially rural areas.

🔧 What We Offer

  • Passenger rides (car, bike, e-rickshaw)
  • Goods transport (tractor & load vehicles)
  • Driver-on-demand services

🤝 For Drivers & Owners

We create earning opportunities for local drivers and vehicle owners.

🔐 Safety & Trust

We ensure safety, transparency, and reliable service for all users.

📍 Our Vision

To empower rural communities through smart transportation.
